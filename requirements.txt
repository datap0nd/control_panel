Subject: Dashboard progress update - completed improvements and remaining decisions

Hi all,

Here is a summary of the dashboard improvements completed over the last few days and the decisions still required.

Completed:

- Prorated every monthly Sales Plan value to match the days included in the actual data. A half-month of actuals is now compared with a half-month of Plan instead of the full monthly target.
- Corrected the Sales KPI period behavior. Clearing the Period filter was silently forcing a YTD result; it now shows the complete available Sales history and avoids showing misleading Plan or prior-year comparisons without a selected period.
- Corrected the Executive monthly charts. They were repeating cumulative values at each month, which made trends appear flat or overstated; each point now represents that individual month.
- Aligned current and prior-year KPI comparisons to the exact selected dates rather than comparing broad calendar-month groups.
- Restricted Sales Plan comparisons to the level supported by the Plan source. Unsupported channel, product, device, payment, and partner views now return no comparison instead of a misleading target.
- Corrected the current Active Policies KPI so it shows the current active stock rather than every policy that was active at any time during the selected period.
- Rebuilt the historical Active Policies trend as true month-end snapshots. Policies that closed later are now still counted in the months when they were active.
- Corrected Active Monthly Subscribers to represent active subscribers at month-end rather than monthly sales or revenue activity.
- Aligned the paid-monthly subscriber history with the confirmed launch timing, preventing comparisons against periods when the offer did not yet exist.
- Reworked the cancellation rate so monthly cancellation events are compared with the appropriate opening subscriber base and then combined consistently across selected months.
- Corrected Policy date handling so source dates are interpreted in the intended format and do not shift between days and months.
- Standardized Policy statuses into clear Active, Expired, Cancelled, and Review groups so variations in source wording no longer change the dashboard classification.
- Separated partner reporting from Sales reporting. Because the Sales sources do not contain a partner, account, or store field, partner views now use New Policies as an explicitly separate proxy rather than being presented as Sales.
- Corrected the Sales-to-Policy reconciliation after confirming that Turkey is intentionally outside Policy coverage, so the comparison now uses an aligned country scope.
- Reviewed the Attach Rate grain across Sales and Sellout and limited the comparison to dimensions supported by both sources, avoiding unsupported account or store-level matching.
- Removed B2B as a separate Plan-mapping issue because the approved actual-versus-Plan comparison does not match individual Sales channels.
- Corrected the September source header so the Plan month loads consistently with the other months.

Still pending:

- Confirm the ERP scope: Gross or Net actuals, reporting currency, latest closed month, Plan version, and whether the view should cover the full subsidiary or only the mapped program.
- Confirm whether SES can be separated from Other in Sellout. Until that mapping is reliable, the E-Store plus SES Attach actual-versus-Plan comparison should remain unpublished.
- Complete the final live-dashboard validation of the Period filter behavior across KPI cards and charts.
- Refresh the Information page with the confirmed definitions and complete the final dashboard quality review.

Once the remaining business decisions are confirmed, the dashboard can move to final sign-off.
