SC+ TO GSCM PARTNER MAPPING

Confirmed scope
- The stakeholder request covers only SC+ and GSCM.
- Do not discuss downstream systems, dashboard implementation, or other
  mapping layers in the request.
- Hyeonmin and Zouhir are jointly responsible for validating the same SC+ to
  GSCM mapping.

Objective
Map the SC+ partner names in Policy to the GSCM account master.

What was attempted
1. Exact GSCM Sold To ID matches.
2. Exact account-name matches.
3. Partner-name and Sold To name matches.
4. Grouping multiple GSCM stores under one SC+ partner.

Why manual validation is required
1. The SC+ partner-code field is blank in the Policy data. The only available
   partner-level identifier is partner_name.
2. SC+ and GSCM often use different names for the same partner. Some SC+
   values are abbreviations, numeric values, operational labels, or grouped
   partner names.
3. One SC+ partner may represent several GSCM stores, accounts, or Sold To
   IDs. Some SC+ values represent a complete channel instead of one partner.
4. Some GSCM account names are generic or shared by multiple partners.

Required deliverable
Provide one Excel mapping table with the following columns:

- SC+ Country
- SC+ Channel
- SC+ Partner Name
- GSCM Country
- GSCM Sales Channel
- GSCM Account Name
- GSCM Sold To ID

Use one row per mapping relationship. If one SC+ partner represents multiple
GSCM accounts, include one row for every GSCM account.

Stakeholder request
Hyeonmin and Zouhir should review the proposed relationships and complete or
correct the unresolved SC+ to GSCM mappings.

Communication requirements
- Keep the message short and use simple English.
- Organize it under: objective, what was attempted, challenges, and requested
  output.
- Ask for one action only: return the completed Excel mapping table.
